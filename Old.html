SAiNTS - 2026

Prioritizing sustainable research is essential to tackle global challenges like climate change and resource depletion. Fostering innovation that balances economic, social, and environmental needs is needed to bolster long-term well-being for all. Advancing scientific research focused on sustainability creates a resilient and harmonious relationship between human activities and the planet, leading to a healthier and more equitable world.

In this regard, CHRIST (Deemed to be University) is happy to welcome you to the international conference on Scientific Advances In Natural and Technological Sciences (SAiNTS - 2026) providing an excellent platform for experts and researchers to share knowledge and exchange ideas so as to understand and address the challenges that lie ahead for a sustainable future

About CHRIST

CHRIST (Deemed to be University) was established as ‘Christ College’ in 1969. It undertook path-breaking initiatives in Indian higher education with the introduction of innovative and modern curricula, insistence on academic discipline, imparting of Holistic Education and adoption of global higher education practices with the support of creative and dedicated staff.

One of the first institutions in India to be accredited in 1998 by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC), Government of India, and subsequently in 2004, 2016, 2022 CHRIST (Deemed to be University) is currently accredited with ‘A+’ Grade. The University is ranked among top 100 universities in India at 67 in the NIRF India Ranking 2023 of Ministry of Education, Government of India.

The campus is a living example of harmonious multiculturalism with students from all the states and union territories and around 60 different countries. CHRIST (Deemed to be University) publishes six peer-reviewed research journals and has published more than 300 books in Kannada and English. A promoter of sports, music and literary activities, it is a nurturing ground for creative excellence.

It is with great pleasure that I extend a warm welcome to each of you at the CHRIST (Deemed to be University) SAINTS-"International Conference: Scientific Advances In Natural and Technological Sciences". As the Vice Chancellor of CHRIST (Deemed to be University), I am honored to witness the convergence of brilliant minds from diverse fields, collectively contributing to the advancement of knowledge and innovation.

Our commitment to fostering excellence in education and research is unwavering. SAINTS-"International Conference: Scientific Advances in Natural Sciences & Techniques" serves as a platform to exchange groundbreaking ideas, explore collaborative research initiatives, and nurture the next generation of thought leaders. By bringing together experts from various disciplines, we aim to create an environment that transcends traditional boundaries and encourages interdisciplinary collaboration.

In addition to academic pursuits, we recognize the pivotal role of industry and research laboratories in driving innovation. This conference seeks to establish and strengthen connections between academia and industry, fostering partnerships that bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application. Through such collaborations, we aspire to contribute meaningfully to societal progress and address the challenges of our time.

Networking is a cornerstone of progress, and this conference provides a unique opportunity to forge connections at both national and international levels. By building bridges across borders, we hope to create a global community of scholars and professionals dedicated to advancing education, research, and innovation.

I am confident that the ideas generated and relationships formed during this conference will have a lasting impact on the landscape of education and research. I encourage each participant to actively engage in the discussions, share insights, and explore avenues for collaboration.

In Advance, thank you for your presence and active participation. Together, let us embark on a journey of intellectual exploration and collaborative discovery.

Destination

Bengaluru, often hailed as the "Silicon Valley of India," stands as a bustling metropolis and the capital of Karnataka. This city is a technological powerhouse, serving as the epicenter of India's information technology industry. Renowned for its numerous tech parks, startup culture, and multinational corporations, Bengaluru attracts a global pool of talent and investment. Beyond its technological prowess, Bengaluru offers a rich blend of cultures and traditions. The city seamlessly combines its historic charm, exemplified by landmarks like the Vidhana Soudha, with the modernity reflected in its vibrant shopping districts and contemporary architecture. Cubbon Park, a sprawling green oasis in the heart of the city, provides a refreshing escape from the urban bustle. Bengaluru's moderate climate, dynamic workforce, and diverse culinary scene further contribute to its allure, making it a compelling destination for both business and leisure.
